# Waste Collection Schedule - Kitchener

## Garbage Collection
Garbage is collected weekly on your designated collection day. Place bins at the curb by 7:00 AM. 

## Blue Box (Recycling)
Blue box recycling is collected every other week. Place at curb by 7 AM on your collection day.

## Green Cart (Organics)
Green cart organics collection is weekly. Green carts must be at the curb by 7 AM.

## Large Items
Large items are collected by appointment. Call 519-741-2345 to schedule.

## Collection Days by Area
- Monday: Alpine, Laurentian, Westmount
- Tuesday: Forest Heights, Victoria Hills
- Wednesday: Downtown, Cedar Hill
- Thursday: Doon, Idlewood
- Friday: Fairway, Bridgeport
