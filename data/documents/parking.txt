# Parking in Kitchener

## Pay Parking Ticket
Pay online at kitchener.ca/paytickets
Pay by phone: 519-741-2345
Pay in person: City Hall, 200 King St W

## Parking Permits
Residential parking permits available for $30/year.
Apply online at kitchener.ca/parking

## Parking Violations
- Overtime parking: $30
- No parking zone: $60
- Fire route: $300
- Handicapped parking: $300

## Payment Deadline
Payment is due within 30 days of ticket issue date.
Late payment fee of $10 applies after 30 days.

## Dispute a Ticket
File an online appeal at kitchener.ca/parking within 14 days.
