# Building Permits - Kitchener

## When You Need a Permit
Building permits are required for:
- New construction
- Additions over 100 sq ft
- Renovations over $10,000
- Deck or fence over 6 ft
- Swimming pools
- Demolition

## How to Apply
1. Apply online at kitchener.ca/permits
2. Submit required documents
3. Pay permit fees
4. Wait for approval (10-15 business days)

## Required Documents
- Completed permit application
- Site plan
- Building plans
- Contractor info (if applicable)
- Proof of ownership

## Permit Fees
Fees based on project value:
- $0-$10,000: $200
- $10,001-$50,000: $300 + $10 per $1,000 over $10,000
- $50,000+: $700 + $8 per $1,000 over $50,000

## Inspection Required
All permits require inspections at various stages:
- Foundation
- Framing
- Electrical
- Plumbing
- Final
