# Municipal Chatbot Lead Research - Kitchener/Waterloo

**Research Date:** March 2025  
**Objective:** Find warm leads at Kitchener/Waterloo municipalities to validate willingness to pay for municipal AI chatbot.

---

## Key Insight: Both Cities Are Already Exploring AI!

**Critical Finding:** Both City of Kitchener and City of Waterloo are actively exploring AI/chatbot solutions. Kitchener has already piloted a chatbot with Waterloo AI Institute and recently partnered with municiPal AI (a startup). This shows strong willingness to explore - but also existing vendor relationships.
